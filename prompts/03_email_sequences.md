## 3.1 5步跟进序列（从首封到放弃）

### 步骤1：首封邮件（Day 0）

```
Subject: [他们的公司/痛点] + [我能提供的价值]

Body:
Hi [Name],

I noticed [SPECIFIC_OBSERVATION_ABOUT_THEIR_COMPANY]. Most companies in [THEIR_INDUSTRY] struggle with [PAIN_POINT].

We've helped companies like [SIMILAR_COMPANY] achieve [QUANTIFIABLE_RESULT] in [TIMEFRAME]. Here's how it works:

[1-2 SENTENCE_DESCRIPTION_OF_YOUR_SOLUTION]

[ONE_SENTENCE_SOCIAL_PROOF]

Would it make sense to explore this in a quick 15-minute call this week?

[Your Name]
```

---

### 步骤2：3天后（Day 3）

```
Subject: Re: [原邮件主题] - Quick Follow-up

Just circling back on my note from [DATE]. I know you're probably swamped, so I'll keep this short.

Quick question: Is [SPECIFIC_QUESTION_ABOUT_THEIR_SITUATION] something you're currently working on?

If the timing isn't right, totally understand. Just let me know where you are and I'll adjust my follow-up accordingly.

Best,
[Your Name]

P.S. [TIME-SENSITIVE_VALUE_ADD] - [具体内容，如"我们这周有首单优惠"或"本周五前报名可获得免费咨询"]
```

## 3.2 拒绝应对序列 - "价格太高"

### 场景：客户说价格太高

```
Subject: Re: Your pricing

Hi [Name],

I hear you. And honestly, [COMPETITOR] probably looks cheaper on paper.

Here's what I'd ask you to consider:

1. [Hidden cost of competitor - e.g., "Their MOQ is 3x ours, so you're paying for inventory you don't need"]

2. [Quality difference - e.g., "Our defect rate is <0.5% vs industry average of 3% - what's that cost you in returns?"]

3. [Total cost of ownership - e.g., "When you factor in our 2-year warranty vs their 3-month, the math changes"]

I've had clients come to us after switching to cheaper options. Almost always, they spent more in the long run.

I'm not saying we're right for everyone. But I'd hate for you to make a decision based on sticker price alone.

What would it take to make this work at [PRICE THEY SAID THEY'D PAY]?

[Your Name]
```

```
Which of these makes the most sense for your situation?

[Your Name]
```

## 3.3 拒绝应对序列 - "供应商已经定了"

```
Subject: Re: Vendor situation

Hi [Name],

Totally understand. Finding a new vendor is a big decision, and you've probably already done that work.

Here's the thing though - I hear this from a lot of companies right before they switch:

"We didn't realize how much [PROBLEM - e.g., quality issues/delays/price hikes] was costing us until it hit critical mass."

I'm not suggesting you drop everything. But what if we stayed in touch for when things change?

I'll check in every [FREQUENCY - e.g., quarter/6 months]. No sales pressure, just [SOMETHING_VALUABLE - e.g., market updates/product samples/relevant insights].

Would that be useful?

[Your Name]

P.S. [COMPELLING_REASON_TO_RECONSIDER] - e.g., "We're actually taking on 2 new clients this month for our [NEW_PRODUCT_LINE] - might be worth a conversation when you have 10 minutes"
```

## 3.4 催单邮件序列

### 催单序列A - 正常推进中客户

```
Day 0 - 报价后3天：
Subject: Any questions on the quote? 🤔

Hi [Name],

Just checking in on the quote I sent on [DATE]. 

I know there's a lot to review. A few clients have asked me the same questions, so I compiled answers here: [LINK TO FAQ]

Happy to jump on a quick call if that's easier.

[Your Name]
```

```
Day 7 - 未回复：
Subject: Following up on [PRODUCT]

Hi [Name],

Haven't heard from you since I sent the quote on [DATE]. 

Just wanted to make sure it didn't get buried. A few updates on our end:

- [UPDATE 1 - e.g., "We've received several orders from [REGION] this month"]
- [UPDATE 2 - e.g., "Lead time is currently [X] weeks due to demand"]

Still very interested in working together. What's the best way to move forward?

[Your Name]
```

```
Day 14 - 最后通牒：
Subject: Order timeline 📅

Hi [Name],

I want to be upfront: I have another client interested in [PRODUCT], and I need to give them an answer by [DATE].

To be fair to both of you, I want to understand where things stand:

1. Is this still a priority for you?
2. What's the decision timeline?
3. Is there anything holding up the decision?

If this isn't going to work out, I'd rather know now so I can move forward with the other client. But if there's still genuine interest, let's find a path forward.

[Your Name]
```

## 使用说明

1. 邮件序列要有节奏感 - 不要连发
2. 每封邮件只focus在一个目标
3. 价值递增 - 每封邮件都要比上一封提供更多价值
4. 给客户"出口" - 让他们知道如何告诉你"不"
5. 真正的个性化 > 模板修改
6. 发送时间：周二/周三/周四上午效果最好
