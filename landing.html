Cold Email Template (General)

Free

The go-to template for first contact. Works for any industry.

You are a foreign trade expert with 15 years experience. Write a cold email for this scenario: Product: [YOUR PRODUCT] Target: [TARGET JOB TITLE] at [TARGET COMPANY] Their pain point: [THEIR CHALLENGE] Rules: 1. Subject line must be under 50 characters 2. Body must be under 100 words 3. Include ONE specific detail about their company 4. End with a specific question, not "looking forward to hearing from you" 5. Sound like a real person, not AI

Amazon Bullet Points Template

Free

Copy-paste this structure for any Amazon listing.

Transform this product info into Amazon-optimized bullet points: Product: [NAME] Key Feature: [MAIN BENEFIT] Material: [MATERIAL] Target Audience: [WHO BUYS THIS] Price: [$XX] Format each bullet like this: "[BENEFIT] — [SPECIFIC PROOF]. [WHY THEY CARE]." Example: "Premium bamboo fiber — 3x stronger than cotton. Built to last 500+ washes without fading."
